About MythiStone

Data-driven meta-analysis for World of Warcraft players.

MythiStone produces tier lists and tools so players can make informed decisions. We aggregate leaderboard data to rank specializations, dungeon routes, items, talents and more.

Mission

Provide clear, timely and transparent recommendations.

Values
  • Player-first recommendations
  • Open data & clear methods
How we build our data

Our methodology is designed to use automated data collection and analysis. We aim to be transparent about sources and calculations.

  • Collect Blizzard leaderboard data.
  • Compute performance metrics from the last two weeks.

Contribute & Report

Found an issue in data or a tier placement you disagree with? Help us improve:

  1. Check existing issues on the repo and search for similar reports.
  2. Open a new issue with as much information as possible.
  3. Or drop a message in Discord with relevant links/screenshots.
